Our Story

Vanilla Alchemy is the heart of WHISK — a collection built on the belief that vanilla is magic. We transform brown butter, caramel, spices, and fragrant vanilla beans into luxurious cookies crafted in small batches. Every ingredient, scrap, and crumble is repurposed into new creations to honor a zero-waste, all-flavour philosophy.
